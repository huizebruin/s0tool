S0tool Overview

The S0tool is a versatile tool for monitoring water and energy consumption. It seamlessly integrates with ESPHome and offers various features.

Key Features

  • Keeps track of water and energy consumption with ESPHome and Home Assistant
  • Ideal for monitoring devices such as heat pumps or solar panel yields
  • Transforms a non-smart kWh meter with an S0 port into a smart meter

S0tool Benefits

With the S0tool, you can easily monitor your water and energy consumption in real-time. This helps you identify areas for improvement and make informed decisions about your energy usage.

Made for ESPHome

The S0tool has an official Made for ESPHome license, ensuring seamless integration and reliable performance.
