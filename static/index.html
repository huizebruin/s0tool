About S0tool

The S0tool is a versatile tool for monitoring water and energy consumption. It seamlessly integrates with ESPHome and offers various features to help you track and optimize your usage.

Compatible Water Meters

The S0tool works with a variety of water meters that support NPN sensors. Below is a list of compatible water meters:
