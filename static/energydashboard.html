Watermeter and S0 Port in Energy Dashboard

From Home Assistant version 2022.11.X, it is possible to add the water meter to the energy dashboard with the S0tool from version v22.10.20. You can also use the S0 port (D5) for heating units, solar power, or units with an S0 port.

Water & Energy Dashboard Dashboard

Look for watermeter stand and add it to the water usage in the energy dashboard. Look for Totaal opgebracht and add it to the S0 port usage in the energy dashboard.

How to Change the Total Readings

For the water counter: D2

For the S0 port of the kWh meter: D5

You can find your readings and reset them.
