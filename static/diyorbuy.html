Build It Yourself

Wemos D1 mini + NPN proximity sensor + 10KΩ resistor. Total cost under €10.

  1. Connect one end of the 10KΩ resistor to 5V.
  2. Connect the other end to D2.
  3. Connect D2 to the signal wire of the NPN sensor.
  4. Connect the GND wire of the NPN sensor to GND.
Note: Currently only works with Wemos D1 mini V1 or V3.0 (ESP8266). You also need a USB cable and 5V/1A adapter.

kWh Meter Integration

  1. Ensure your kWh meter has an S0 output port.
  2. Connect a 10KΩ resistor between GND and D5.
  3. Connect 5V to port 20 of the S0 terminal.
  4. Connect D5 to port 21 of the S0 terminal.
