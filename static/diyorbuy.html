DIY Water Counter

To create a DIY water counter, you'll need specific components like the Wemos D1 mini, an NPN sensor, and a 10K resistor. The 10K resistor should be connected between the 5V and D2, and from D2 to the NPN sensor.

kWh Meter Integration

The S0tool can also be used to monitor your kWh meter. This allows you to track your energy consumption in real-time and integrate it into Home Assistant for better energy management.

To connect your kWh meter:

  1. Ensure your kWh meter has an S0 output port.
  2. Connect the S0 output to the S0tool using the appropriate wiring.
  3. Configure the S0tool in ESPHome or Home Assistant to read the pulses from the kWh meter.
