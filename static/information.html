S0tool Information

Everything you need to know about setting up and using the S0tool.

Technical Information

  • Wi-Fi: IEEE 802.11 b/g/n 2.4GHz
  • Minimal ESPHome version: 2024.12.x
  • Minimal Home Assistant version: 2025.01.x
  • Works only for a Wemos D1 mini V1, V3.0 ESP8266 at this moment.

Required components:

  • Wemos D1 mini ESP8266: AliExpress, Amazon
  • NPN Sensor: AliExpress, Amazon, Opencircuit
  • 10K Resistor: AliExpress, Amazon, Opencircuit

Additionally, you will need a USB cable and a USB power adapter with at least 5V and 1A.
