Installation

Flash S0tool firmware directly from your browser. Requires Chrome or Edge and a USB cable.

Before you start: Install USB drivers for your Wemos D1 mini. See Troubleshooting if your device is not detected.

Step-by-step

  1. Select the configuration matching your hardware below.
  2. Connect your Wemos D1 mini via USB to your PC.
  3. Click Connect — select the USB serial port.
  4. Choose Install S0tool, then click Install.
  5. If it fails, hold the boot button on the device and try again.
  6. After flashing, connect to your 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi network.
Done! Home Assistant will auto-discover the S0tool under Settings → Devices & Services.
